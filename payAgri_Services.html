At payAgri Global, we are dedicated to transforming agriculture through sustainability, inclusivity, and innovation. With operations spanning India, Africa, and the UAE, we empower farmers, food processors, and MSMEs by enhancing market access, facilitating value addition, and driving financial inclusion. Our services are structured around four key pillars:
Farmer Level Support
We collaborate closely with farmers and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) to source premium spices, food grains, and export-oriented perishables that meet international quality standards. Our farmer-focused initiatives include:
Market Access: Connecting farmer-grown products to global markets.
Financial Support: Providing easy access to credit, crop insurance, and daily hospital cash coverage.
Sustainable Farming: Promoting responsible agricultural practices to ensure long-term viability and growth.
Through these initiatives, we enhance farmers income stability and improve their overall well-being.
Food Processor Solutions
We support food processors with state-of-the-art facilities that ensure premium quality processing of agri-products like cardamom, pepper, turmeric, chili, sweet corn, and coconut copra. Our solutions include:
Cloud Processing Model: Enabling small and large food businesses to scale efficiently.
Advanced Technology: Ensuring high-quality processing with cutting-edge techniques.
Supply Chain Integration: Collaborating with global partners to maintain consistency and efficiency.
By bridging gaps in the supply chain, we empower food processors to expand their reach and enhance their product offerings.
Global Market Linkage
payAgri Global facilitates seamless international trade, ensuring that farm produce and processed food products reach global consumers. Our market linkage services include:
Import-Export & Bulk Supply: Helping businesses expand into new international markets.
Retail Distribution: Connecting producers with global retail channels.
Market Expansion: Strengthening our presence in Africa, the UAE, and beyond to create more growth opportunities.
We streamline the farm-to-market journey, ensuring smooth access to international trade and sustained business growth.
Food-Ag consulting and projects
Our specialized consulting services cater to farmer groups, food processors, and MSMEs, addressing challenges in financial linkage, market access, and operational efficiency. Our consulting expertise spans:
Business & Investment Advisory: Assisting with fundraising and investment strategies.
Farm Management Solutions: Enhancing productivity and sustainability at the farm level.
EPC & Climate Solutions: Providing innovative solutions for environmental resilience.
Technology Consulting: Integrating advanced technology into agribusiness operations.
By offering strategic guidance and tailored solutions, we help businesses optimize their value chains, increase efficiency, and unlock new growth avenues.
Building a Sustainable Future
At payAgri Global, we are committed to fostering a future where farmers, MSMEs, and food processors thrive within a globally connected agricultural ecosystem. Join us as we revolutionize agriculture, drive economic growth, and create lasting impact worldwide.

The Provimi Transformation
In March 2024, PayAgri Global took a decision to make a strategic investment in Gobi based Provimi Products pvt Ltd, a well-known brand in climate friendly livestock nutrition with a strong 40-year legacy. Like many businesses, Provimi was affected by the challenges brought on by the pandemic and was in need of support to get back on its track.
PayAgri stepped in not just with funds, but with a clear strategy to rebuild and grow the business. We renovated the factory, improved operations, regained the confidence of customers & employees, worked closely with the team to bring structure to sales and marketing and created many strategic partnership. We also supported fund-raising, helping Provimi regain financial stability and focus.
As part of the transformation, Provimi introduced a renewed focus on bio and herbal-based livestock feed including cattle, Goat, Poultry & Fish an approach that not only supports animal health but also contributes to sustainability by helping reduce methane emissions.
Today, Provimi is on a steady path of growth, regaining market confidence and preparing for wider expansion across India and beyond. This journey highlights the impact of our timely support and strategic direction in bringing a business back to life.
At PayAgri Global, we are driven by more than financial returns, we invest in potential, nurture meaningful progress, and aim to create enduring impact.
